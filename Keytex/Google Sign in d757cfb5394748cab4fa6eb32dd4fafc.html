Android (DEV KEY)

After adding firebase you need to add the android debugger key(finger print) to the google package on firebase

Generate the key using and redownload and override the current google-services.json file

Ensure you replace codememory with you username

Wondows


keytool -list -v -keystore "C:\Users\codememeory\.android\debug.keystore" -alias androiddebugkey -storepass android -keypass android

Mac/Linux


keytool -list -v -keystore "/Users/codememory/.android/debug.keystore" -alias androiddebugkey -storepass android -keypass android

Android (LIVE KEY)

Go to google play console and selected your app and copy the SHA-1 certificate fingerprint and SHA-256 certificate fingerprint key and update in firebase like above
